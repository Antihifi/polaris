# NPC System

**Status:** Not Yet Implemented

See [docs/DESIGN.md](../../docs/DESIGN.md#npc-system) for full design specification.

## Quick Summary

- Inuit camps for trade and survival knowledge
- Barter-based trading (no currency)
- Reputation system affecting NPC disposition
- Simple branching dialogue

## Design Principles

- Cultural sensitivity - Inuit portrayed respectfully as survival experts
- NPCs provide alternative strategies, information, hope, moral choices
